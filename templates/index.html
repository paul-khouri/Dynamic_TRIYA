Who we are

The Academy provides a range of yachting opportunities to young college students interested in sailing. TRIYA’s aim is to make sailing accessible to all school age students to encourage a love for the water and sailing.

We run programs from beginner levels all the way to national champions.

420 Sailing

Based in Evans Bay, Wellington. The Academy provides a range of yachting opportunities to young college students interested in sailing. We offer Learn to Sail, Teams Racing and Fleet Racing programs.

The Academy owns a number of international 420 class boats for use by secondary students wishing to pursue teams racing, learn to sail & fleet racing.
